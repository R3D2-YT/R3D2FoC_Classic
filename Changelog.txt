0.5 (Current Version)

Massively improved visuals:

 - New models for almost all base game (space) units
 - New models for galactic planets
 - Improved shaders
 - New galactic map background

New Units for all factions:

 - Empire:
   - Tector Class Star Destroyer
   - TIE Avenger Squadron
   - Ton Falk Carrier

 - Rebellion:
   - MC80 Home One Type

 - Zahn Consortium:
   - A4 Juggernaut
   - B1 Battle Droid
   - B2 Battle Droid

Misc Changes:

 - The Death Star and Death Star II are both avalilable to the Empire at Tech 5 (with a level 5 starbase on the Maw and Endor respectively)
   - The price of both Death Stars has been massively raised

 - The Executor can only be built on Kuat or Fondor

 - Victory Star destroyer now no longer carries TIE Bombers or Interceptors, now only carries TIE Fighters

 - Most fighters can now make use of the "Hunt For Enemies" ability

 - The Zahn Consortium can now build Z95 Headhunter Squadrons
